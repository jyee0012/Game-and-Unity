Space Invaders Level - worth 15% of your final mark.

Create one level of "space invaders".

Complete the following requirements to earn a maximum of 20 marks.

-   (4 marks) Set up a space invaders game. The player ship will be on the bottom and the alien ships will be at the top. It should contain:
        a player ship that can move left and right
        many alien ships that move left and right
-   (2 marks) The alien ships should change direction and move down one ship length when they hit either side of level.
-   (4 marks) Give the alien ships projectiles. They will shoot every so often, randomly. 
-       (2 marks) If the projectile hits the player ship, the player ship will be destroyed.
-   (4 marks) Give the player ship a projectile. If the player presses the space bar, the projectile shoots upwards. 
-       (2 marks) If the projectile hits an alien ship,  the alien ship will be destroyed.
-?  (2 marks) Set up barriers that stop alien projectiles that the player ship can hide behind.
-       *Challenge* Have the barriers change colours the more they are hit by enemy projectiles. Once they have been hit enough, they disappear.
