For this project you will be creating a simple game that four players can control aspects of a single vehicle. The first player will control the movement of the vehicle and a single forward facing weapon. The second player will control a weapon that fire in any direction like a turret. The third and fourth players will control weapons that can fire 180 degrees to the left or right of the vehicle.

If you wish to work in pairs you must also complete the following tasks: (2 Marks each)

- Add health to the player vehicle. The game ends when destroyed.

- Include at least 5 waves of enemies.

- Keep score of the enemies destroyed and display the score at the end.

- Allow for two player vehicles with two players each. This mode is chosen at the player select screen.



Solo Project: 16 Marks

Paired Project: 24 Marks
